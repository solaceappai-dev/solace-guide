Product Guide
Home

Everything at a glance

Your home is a calm dashboard, not a wall of to-dos. Your Solace Score shows at a glance how ready your plan is — weighted toward the things that most ease your family's burden — and a single Continue card always points to your next step, so you never have to wonder where to start.

  • Your Solace Score (0–100) with a gentle band — Getting started to Fully prepared — tap it for what's in place and what to do next
  • One clear next action, with the reason it matters
  • An Ask Solace bar — your AI companion, one tap away
  • A snapshot grid that jumps you into any part of your plan
Plan

All your planning, in one place

The Plan tab gathers everything that needs organizing, grouped so it never feels overwhelming. Work through it at your own pace — Solace tracks your progress as you go.

  • A guided checklist across four phases: planning ahead, immediate, first week, first month
  • Documents & records, with an end-to-end encrypted vault
  • Estate & asset inventory, digital accounts
  • Final wishes, letters to loved ones, cultural customs & crisis notes
Companion

An AI companion, in your corner

Solace's companion meets you where you are. It opens with a gentle check-in and offers guided prompts for the moments that are hard to start — then drafts, organizes, and explains, at your pace.

  • Warm, judgment-free check-ins
  • Guided prompts: tell the family, write the obituary, get organized
  • Understands the context of your plan and your loved one
  • Powered by Claude — Opus on Pro, always private
Family

Share with the people you trust

Plan for more than one person, and give trusted people access — on your terms, when it's needed. Solace keeps a clear record of who can see what.

  • Switch between family-member profiles
  • Invite deputies (read-only) or collaborators (read & write)
  • A check-in so access stays in your hands
  • An activity log of every access and change

FAQ

Questions, answered

The things people ask most before they begin.

Do I need an account?
No. Solace works locally on your device from the first tap. Sign in only when you want cloud sync across devices or to share with family.
Is my information private?
Yes. When you sign in, your plan syncs end-to-end encrypted — only you hold the key. Sensitive documents live in an encrypted vault, and we never sell your data.
What does it cost?
Solace is free to start, with a Pro trial included. Pro unlocks the full AI companion, the encrypted vault, estate inventory and more; a Family plan adds profiles and sharing.
Is this legal or financial advice?
No. Solace helps you organize and find footing — it isn't a substitute for an attorney or financial advisor. For legal matters, please consult a qualified professional.
Can I share my plan with family?
Yes. Invite trusted people as read-only deputies or full collaborators, switch between family-member profiles, and review an activity log of every access and change.
What happens when it's needed?
A gentle check-in keeps access in your hands, and posthumous-release settings make sure your plan reaches the people you've trusted at the right time.
